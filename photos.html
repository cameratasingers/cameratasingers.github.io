1980

Carol (Ptack) Ramsey, Music Director - Photo taken at a Lake Forest Symphony benefit.

  
 
The Camerata Singers performed only one concert with women singers only. In early 1981, the women were joined by men singers and this has been the Camerata lineup since that time. From left - Carol (Ptack) Ramsey (director), Susan (Cartland) Bode, Ruth Harris, Rae Kendrick, Virginia Long-Cecil, Eleanor Kraemer, Claudia Kerski, and Joan Shelton.
