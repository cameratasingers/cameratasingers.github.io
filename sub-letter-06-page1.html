The Board of the Camerata Singers of Lake Forest is proud to present the 2006/2007 concert schedule.  Music Director/Conductor Julia Davids has pulled together an exciting and entertaining group of concerts.  We congratulate Julia on achieving her Doctor of Music degree from Northwestern University this June.  Similar to eighteenth century Europe, concerts are presented in "cameras" (the Italian word for "chamber") where the singers and audience can interact in a very special way.   The wonderfully spacious homes of the Lake Forest area are ideally suited for this presentation style.  For more information on Camerata, including descriptions of past programs, photos of singers and previous concert sites, please visit our website, www.cameratasingers.org   The ticket price includes an elegant reception with wine, appetizers and desserts.  All concerts are on Sunday afternoons, with performances at 2:30 p.m. and 5:00 p.m. unless stated otherwise.   Guests for the 5:00 p.m. concert arrive at 4:00 p.m. to participate in the reception.  This year’s series will consist of three concerts as follows –

 

 

Sunday, December 3rd, 2006 – A Very Camerata Christmas  - We begin the season with a wonderfully eclectic mix of Christmas music, Camerata style!  German baroque composer Hasse’s delightful “Magnificat” will feature soloists and pianist Janet Eckhardt.  Two contemporary carols by women composers are sure to touch your hearts.  Traditional carols with a new twist include “Deck the Halls” (in 7/8) and “Silent Night” as recorded by the Singers Unlimited.  And it wouldn’t be Christmas with Camerata without carols from other countries, spirituals and a little jazz!  Let us wish you “A Very Camerata Christmas!”

 

Sunday, February 11th, 2007 – Love! Love! Love!  – Featuring baroque violinist Martin Davids and the Callipygian Players on period instruments.  Music Director Julia Davids joins with her ‘love’, husband and baroque violinist Martin Davids, for this much-anticipated program showcasing the various sides of love.  Explore spiritual love through Monteverdi’s exciting “Dixit Dominus” for choir, soloists, two violins and continuo.   Love is often reflected in nature with Mulholland’s “Red, Red Rose” and the two Irish airs “O Danny Boy” and “My Lagan Love”.   Our love for one another has been eloquently expressed in songs by Billy Joel, Gershwin and others.  The Camerata Singers demonstrate their love for singing for you with this passionate program.  This concert will have a single performance at 3:00 pm.

 

Sunday, June 10th, 2007 – ‘Til the Fat Lady Sings! - In great Camerata tradition, the Singers will share some of their favorite music from opera, operetta and musical theatre.  You will hear exciting performances by soloists and small ensembles from within the choir.   The full chorus will sing numbers from Purcell’s “King Arthur”, Bizet’s “Carmen”, Offenbach’s “La Jolie Parfumeuse” and Verdi’s “Nabucco” and “La Traviata”.  Enjoy the lighter side of opera with Camerata – the fun won’t be over until the proverbial Fat Lady sings! 

 

The Camerata Experience continues to be one of the best bargains on the North Shore.  Our prices for this year will be $69/person for the full 3-concert series, $50/person for a 2-concert series (you pick the dates) and $28/person for a single concert.    Please enclose your check with your subscription request.
