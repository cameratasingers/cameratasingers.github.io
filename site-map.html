Site Map
This page displays the information available in this web site and, in most cases, will allow you to go directly to any of the topics listed. In some cases, you may have to move the cursor over a specific button after going to the new page.
Advertisers - A listing of advertisers in Camerata's 2007/2008 program brochure. Links are provided (if available) to the advertiser's individual web site. Move the cursor over the word "Advertisers" to view the page
Board of Directors - Listing of the current members of the Camerata Board of Directors. Move the cursor over the word "Board" to view the page
Concert sites - Commercial/Public/Sacred buildings - a collection of photographs/addresses of concert sites that were originally public or commerical buildings and/or houses of worship
Concert sites - Older Homes - a collection of photographs/addresses of concert sites that were originally private homes and were built at least 50 years ago.
Concert Themes - a listing of the names of all Camerata concerts since 1980 sorted into various categories of themes. An image of the song list for each concert is available for viewing by clicking on the name of the concert. Many of the concerts have several one minute sound clips available. If the concert name is preceded by one or more pair of musical notes, you can click on any set of notes to hear representative songs from that concert in .mp3 format.
Concert Venues - A overview of concert venues and photographs and descriptions of selected venues.
Concerts - 2007/2008 - descriptions of the four Camerata concerts scheduled for the 2007/2008 season.
Director - Photograph and bio of Julia Davids, Music Director/Conductor of the Camerata Singers. Move the cursor over the word "Director" to view the page.
History of Camerata - The history of the Camerata Singers from 1980 to the present including a listing of Board Presidents.
Order CDs - a direct link to SoundByte Studios website for ordering CDs of Camerata concerts.
Photo Gallery - a collection of photographs of Camerata Singers from 1980 to current. Names of the singers in each photo are listed under the photo.
Pianist - Photograph and bio of Janet Eckhardt, pianist for the Camerata Singers. Move the cursor over the word "Pianist" to view the page
Season schedule - A listing of the concerts for the 2007/2008 season with dates and brief descriptions of each concert.
Singers - Complete Roster - Alphabetic listing of every singer who has performed in at least one Camerata Singers concert. Each singer is listed along with the number of their concerts, their first concert season and their latest concert season.
Singers - Current Roster - Photographs and short bios of current Camerata Singers. Move the cursor over the word "Singers" to view the page
Sound Clips - An alphabetical index of Camerata sound clips. The listing includes the name of the song, the name of the concert and the composer/arranger. You can click on the name of the song and you will be able to hear the sound clip in .mp3 format.
Sponsors - Photographs and short bios of sponsors for the past two seasons. Move the cursor over the word "Sponsors" to view the page
Subscription letter - a copy of the subscription mailing for the 2007/2008 season.
Subscription response form - a copy of the subscription response form to purchase tickets for the entire 2007/2008 season or for individual concerts
Tickets - Ticket information and prices for the current season.
