Camerata Singers
Complete Roster
  
 
The following alphabetical listing of artists represents all of the people who have performed in various Camerata concerts over the years. The total number of concerts is through the final concert in June 2008. This total is not the number of performances. Many of the concerts consist of two performances and that count would be nearly double the number listed. The "First Year" represents the first season the artist appeared with Camerata and "Last Year" similarly, the latest season the artist appeared. Artists that have performed at least 20 concerts are highlighted.
